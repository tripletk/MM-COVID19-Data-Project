Welcome to the MM COVID-19 Data Project

Hi. My name is Timmy Kyaw, the developer of this project. I observed the lack of statistics and analysis regarding COVID-19 data in Myanmar. Everyday, the cases are still rising yet people are unaware of the severity of the situation. While trying to find graphs and nurmerical numbers of day by day COVID-19 statistics, I was unable to find them. The Myanmar Ministry of Health and Sports is the single source of all COVID-19 data in the country. Simple stats and an amazing ARCgis dashboard are provided on its site however, they are not easily accesible by the public. And so I put together this simplified and visual data project to provide an insight to everyone.
